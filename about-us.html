Who We Are

At CapelaMed, our mission is clear: to make choosing health insurance easier and your life healthier. Led by Gary Danova Chow, a licensed Medicare & Life Agent (CA#: 0784579), we've helped thousands of individuals just like you enroll in the right individual, family, or Medicare plan across California.

We are a team of passionate, caring individuals who are committed to providing you with a great experience. We take the time to get to know you one-on-one, and to hear what matters most to you when selecting health coverage.

Here's what you can expect when you meet with our team.

What We Do

Finding a health plan isn't overwhelming when you have a partner who listens and takes care of you every step of the way. CapelaMed provides you clarity and helps you find the right plan for your unique needs, budget, and lifestyle.

We work with most major insurance companies to make sure you have access to a wide range of reliable coverage options. Plus, you'll enjoy lifelong support – all at no cost or obligation.
