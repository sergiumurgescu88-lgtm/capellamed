SMS Terms & Conditions

By providing your mobile phone number to CapelaMed, you consent to receive text messages (SMS) regarding your Medicare inquiries, appointment reminders, and important updates.

Message frequency varies. You may receive up to 4 messages per month.

You can opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message. You may also contact us at gary@capelamed.online or (949) 628-0207.
