Your Free Consultation Experience

Here's exactly what happens when you book with Gary

1

Schedule Your Call

Book a free 20-minute phone consultation at a time that works for you. No obligation, no pressure.

2

Personalized Review

Gary will ask about your health needs, budget, doctors, and medications to understand what's important to you.

3

Plan Recommendations

Gary will present 2-3 plan options that match your needs, explaining the pros and cons of each.

4

Enrollment Assistance

Once you choose a plan, Gary will walk you through the enrollment process step by step.

5

Confirmation

You'll receive confirmation of your enrollment and all plan materials in the mail.

6

Ongoing Support

Gary is available year-round for questions, claims help, and annual plan reviews.
